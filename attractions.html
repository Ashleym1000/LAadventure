Los Angeles Attractions

Visit some of the most popular locations in Los Angeles.

Universal Studios

Visit Universal Studios where there is much to offer from the Theme Park to City Walk. Great dining location, bars and much more. Don't miss out on Horror Nights. This attraction is for all age groups, from kids, to seniors.

Hollywood Sign

Get some fresh air and venture out for some of the greatest site seeing in your very own, Los Angeles. Grab a friend and a sports bag to take in the beautiful mountains that oversee the city.

Walt Disney Concert Hall

Pamper yourself and dust off that little black dress for a night out at a concert. Get lost in the concert hall where your ears will thank you.
